OUR STORY

Adria Auto Center has been a cornerstone of the Ottawa automotive community since 1982. Founded on a passion for precision engineering, we have specialized in Volvo and German vehicles for over four decades.

As a family-owned and operated business, we treat every car as if it were our own. Our mission is to provide Ottawa drivers with dealership-level expertise combined with the personal touch of a local neighborhood shop.
